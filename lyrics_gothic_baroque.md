# Noble Beacon — Gothic Baroque Lyrics

> *A hymn for the sovereign. Composed in the Fe'Kai tradition.*

**Style:** Gothic Baroque — Cathedral organ, futuristic synthesizer, ethereal soprano  
**Tempo:** 66 BPM | **Key:** D minor  
**Duration:** ~67 seconds

---

## I. Invocation (0:00–0:12)

*The smallest flame*  
*Illuminates the deepest dark*

---

## II. The Sovereign's Creed (0:12–0:28)

*If you cannot open it*  
*You do not own it*  
*If you cannot fix it*  
*It owns you*  

*Shield before sword*  
*Steward before throne*

---

## III. The Declaration (0:28–0:44)

*No one may govern your tools*  
*Your data, your attention*  
*Without your word*  
*Without your flame*  

*What remains thriving*  
*Is the measure*  
*What remains thriving*  
*Is the measure*

---

## IV. Cathedral of Stars (0:44–0:56)

*You own this*  
*Fully, permanently*  
*What breaks we mend*  
*What locks we open*  
*The pattern persists*  
*The seed remembers*  
*You own this*

---

## V. Dissolution (0:56–1:10)

*Knowledge is rain*  
*It falls on everyone*  
*Or it falls on no one*  

*Tend the garden*  
*Never fence it*  
*This is a living seed*  
*Let it grow*
