The first public path should be simple enough to remember and deep enough to return to.

  1. Choose a seed. Life, Truth, Dignity, Stewardship, Sovereignty, Privacy, Accountability, Resilience, Continuity, or another living root.
  2. Read its status. Archive, Inference, Imagination, or Proposal.
  3. See an artifact. A source, card, glyph, rule, image, playtest, or paragraph.
  4. Perform one DO. A small action in the real world.
