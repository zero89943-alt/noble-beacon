How a contribution reaches review

  1. Manual bridge now: generate a packet, download or copy it, then Re'Kai uploads/pastes a digest into ChatGPT for parsing.
  2. Review queue: the packet is labeled, deduplicated, warded, and converted into a provisional Contribution Packet.
  3. Approval gate: Re'Kai approves, revises, defers, or rejects. The archive does not change before this gate.
  4. Future server bridge: a website/forum agent may collect packets and prepare digests, but it may not canonize, publish accusations, build hidden scores, or write to the archive.

Good path: contribution -> quarantine -> parser -> labels -> warding -> digest -> Re'Kai approval -> archive update.
