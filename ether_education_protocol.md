# Noble Beacon: Ether Education Protocol & Strategic Priorities

This document outlines the **Ether Education Protocol** for decentralized learning and identifies the **Critical Infrastructure Priorities** for the Noble Beacon project.

## 1. The Ether Education Protocol (EEP)
The objective is to provide free, high-quality education that is resilient to censorship and accessible via the project's sovereign infrastructure.

### The "Ether" as a Learning Commons
*   **Decentralized Library**: Educational modules are stored across the **Lumen Mesh** and permanent **GPKD** archives.
*   **Local-First Access**: Lessons are served directly from **Steward Nodes** to local devices (pods, laptops, tablets) without requiring an internet connection.
*   **Fe'Kai Grammar of Learning**: Education is structured around the **Five Strands**:
    1.  **Logic and Fallacies** (The Sword of Reason)
    2.  **Music, Rhythm, and Harmony** (Pattern Recognition)
    3.  **Rhetoric and Persuasion Literacy** (Anti-Manipulation)
    4.  **Memory and Spaced Repetition** (The House of Remembering)
    5.  **Craft, Repair, and Land Stewardship** (The Dignity of Making)

### Delivery Mechanisms
*   **The Beacon Broadcast**: Nodes periodically broadcast educational "seed packets" over LoRa or local Wi-Fi hotspots.
*   **The Wrights & Wardens Interface**: Gamified learning modules integrated into the **Kai'Veyr** platform, allowing students to earn "Stewardship Credits" (non-monetary recognition of mastery).
*   **Peer-to-Peer Mentorship**: The **Social Layer** resonance protocols identify local experts to facilitate face-to-face "Garden Gate" learning circles.

## 2. Critical Infrastructure Priorities (Roadmap)

### Priority 1: The LoRa Mesh Nodes (Hardware)
*   **Function**: The backbone of the off-grid communication network.
*   **Goal**: Develop a low-cost, open-source reference design for a LoRa-enabled node that runs the **Lumen** module.
*   **Requirement**: Hardware-level "Real Off" switches for all radios.

### Priority 2: The Browser Extension App (Software)
*   **Function**: A bridge for current internet users to discover and interact with the archive.
*   **Goal**: An "Easter Egg" delivery system that highlights Fe'Kai philosophies and project updates on standard websites.
*   **Requirement**: Zero data collection; operates as a local-only filter/overlay.

### Priority 3: Sovereign Pod Devices (Hardware)
*   **Function**: Personal portable interfaces for the archive and education.
*   **Goal**: A minimalist, E-ink based device designed for reading, writing, and local mesh communication.
*   **Requirement**: No cloud accounts, no biometric tracking, and user-replaceable batteries.

### Priority 4: Medical Modules (Technical/Content)
*   **Function**: Emergency medical and health sovereignty guides.
*   **Goal**: Reconcile the **Book of the Body and Mind** into actionable, offline-first medical protocols.
*   **Requirement**: Focus on "Right to Repair" for the human body, incorporating both modern evidence and traditional resilience practices.

### Priority 5: The Vagus Metronome (Hardware/Biomimetic)
*   **Function**: Physical entrainment device.
*   **Goal**: A wearable or handheld device that uses haptic pulses to assist in nervous system regulation and synchronization with the **Vibe** module.
