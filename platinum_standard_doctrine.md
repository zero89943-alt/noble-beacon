# Economic Harmony: The Platinum Standard (Commodity-Backed)

The Fe'KAI economic model has transitioned from a purely contribution-based virtual system to a **Commodity-Backed Stewardship Model**. This shift anchors the archive's economic resilience in physical reality, primarily through **Platinum** reserves, supplemented by other tangible assets such as gold, silver, and strategic resources. This "Reality is More" approach ensures that the community's value remains sovereign and resilient, even in the face of technological collapse or network instability.

Wealth within this system is governed by the principle of **Stewardship before Ownership**. Reserves are not intended for private accumulation but are held in trust to ensure the continuity of the archive and the collective well-being of the community. By utilizing physical commodities, the archive achieves **Offline Sovereignty**, as the value of its reserves persists independently of any digital infrastructure or external permission systems.

| Economic Component | Implementation Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Reserve** | Physical Platinum, audited and held in stewardship. |
| **Supplementary Assets** | Gold, silver, and strategic resource reserves. |
| **Unit of Value** | Tied to a specific standard weight of physical Platinum. |
| **Mathematical Base** | Base-60 system (60 units = 1 standard reserve weight). |
| **Sovereignty Mode** | Offline-capable, locally recoverable, and resilient. |

The integration of the Platinum Standard into the v0.2 launch ensures that all contributions are anchored in physical integrity. The **Contribute** form has been updated to include fields for commodity verification, allowing "Seed" contributions to be backed by tangible reserves. This reinforces the archive's commitment to protecting future generations from the volatility of unbacked virtual systems.

To maintain the integrity of the reserves, any proposed changes to the economic standard must undergo a rigorous **Warding Pass** by the **Modular AI Council**. This process identifies hidden-power risks or inflationary vulnerabilities before they can affect the archive. As with all critical archive functions, the final authority for reserve allocation and canonical economic updates remains exclusively with **Re'Kai**, ensuring human accountability at the final gate.
