What This Is Not

The Boundary Is Part of the Beacon

This project preserves imagination without allowing imagination to become hidden power.

Not final canon

Public text is labeled. Proposals remain proposals until reviewed.

Not legal citizenship

Civic contributorship is not legal status, nationality, or recognized citizenship.

Not a social score

Contributor labels are path-lanterns, not rank, caste, worth, loyalty, spirituality, or access control.

Not hidden extraction

No dark consent, hidden tracking, manipulative urgency, or AI publication without human review.

The site should not shout. It should stand.

May our works become forests. May our laws become gardens. May our strength become shelter.

No hidden tracking. No hidden scoring. No archive update without Re'Kai approval.
