# Noble Beacon Genome Disclosure Plan

This document outlines the responsible revelation of the **Noble Beacon Genome Branches**, transitioning from hidden lore to actionable civic modules. The disclosure follows a "Source-Led" approach, ensuring that each layer is revealed only when the foundational stewardship is secure.

## 1. The Core Architecture: Living Media
We are moving away from fragile, service-based storage to **Localized Permanence** through a unified hardware-software stack.

### A. The Medium: Hybrid ROM/Flash Cartridge
To handle "everything," the medium utilizes a **Hybrid ROM/Flash Architecture** for speed, security, and durability.
*   **ROM Sector**: Factory-burned base image; immutable and cannot be altered. Holds the core OS and the "First Archive."
*   **Flash Partition**: Secure EEPROM for **Delta-Patching** (append-only) and encrypted local state.

### B. The Format: Glass-Phase Kinetic Disc (GPKD)
*   **Structure**: 120mm disc with an ultra-thin sapphire or quartz crystal lattice wafer.
*   **Static Core (Archival)**: Permanent data written via femtosecond lasers (**5D storage**). Lasts for billions of years.
*   **Dynamic Shell (Rewritable)**: Outer layers doped with phase-change chalcogenide alloy for temporary data/caches.
*   **The Spin Factor**: Sequential read speeds without complex mirror arrays; rotation handles horizontal alignment.

### C. The Transmission: 4D/5D Vectors
*   **Temporal Geometry (4D Vector)**: Data is stored as a geometric progression. The reader interprets the trajectory of structural changes, allowing for flawless reconstruction even if the lattice fractures over millions of years.

## 2. The Civic Nervous System (CNS) Modules
The governance of the archive is modeled after the human autonomic nervous system.

### A. Central Nervous System (CNS): The Spinal Cord
*   **Function**: The main conduit for policy, regulatory standards (tariffs, quality controls), and funding pathways from the governing body to local clinics.

### B. Sympathetic Nervous System (SNS): Emergency Response
*   **EMS & Trauma Networks**: Dispatch algorithms and ambulance fleets optimized for instantaneous reaction.
*   **Pandemic & Disaster Mobilization**: Pre-funded, rapid-scale protocols for deploying field hospitals and strategic stockpiles.
*   **Acute Care**: Real-time triage systems for dynamic ICU bed reallocation.

### C. Parasympathetic Nervous System (PNS): Restoration
*   **Primary & Preventative**: Universal access to routine check-ups, mental health support, and early screenings.
*   **Chronic Disease Management**: Post-acute care and long-term stability programs.
*   **Community Wellness**: Targeting social determinants (clean water, nutrition, fitness spaces).
